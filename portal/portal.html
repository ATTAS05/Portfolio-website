Welcome to GHS Online Result Access Portal!

This portal provides you access to your result 24/7. To check your result, please take the following steps:
  1. Fill in the Card Validation/Activation form below. If you have once filled this form using the same GHS Card PIN, skip and just proceed to Step 2.
  2. Proceed to your left hand corner menu & select a category that best describes you.
  3. Enter your GHS Card PIN, your Admission Number e.g [GHS/09/001], and Select the Academic Session,'proceed to Check Result!'
  4. Print a copy of your result by clicking ‘Print Result’.
